## Engineering principles

- TDD: Test-Driven Development
- YAGNI: You Aren't Gonna Need It
- DAMP: Descriptive and Meaningful Phrases
- DRY: Don't Repeat Yourself
- Focus on local reasoning.

## Scratch space

`docs/superpowers/` is gitignored. Use it for temporary working documents like
implementation plans and design specs. These are ephemeral - durable artifacts
belong in code, tests, or the main `docs/` folder.
